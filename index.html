Meet Stan - The little hero that touched all our hearts ❤️

Stan Parker was an 8-year-old child that was diagnosed with a grade 1 brain tumour that gave him an endless amount of torture, pain that a child so young should not have to endure. We had to take immediate measures that required conduction of a precision-based surgery if we wanted him to be rid of this burden as soon as possible. And with our experienced doctors and the utmost support from our dedicated staff, we were able to get Stan to make a full recovery within the span of 6 months.
We wish Stan the very best in all his future endeavours.
